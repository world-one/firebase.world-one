FAQ list

  • What kind of service is TAP! ?

    TAP! is a mobile service that provides safe movement to the destination by calling an autonomous vehicle with safety drivers. TAP! has acquired a temporary license for autonomous driving from the Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport. Passengers can call and use various autonomous vehicles in the operating area.
